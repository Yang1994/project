    About us

    Hudong Network Technology Co.,Ltd was established in 2016, is one of the leading blockchain application service providers. The company has been committed to providing a quality service to millions of millions of users through its decentralized design to create an autonomous, fair and democratic ecosystem. It has always been in a steady state of development.


    Improving the user's interactive experience based on blockchain is the major mission of Hudong Network Technology Co.,Ltd. At present, The company regards "providing objective and fair innovative application evaluation system to users and developers" as a strategic target. Through decentralized design, users within the ecosystem hold tokens based on blockchains and smart contracts, using their own interactive behaviors to "vote" and spend on applications to create an autonomous, fair and democratic eco-system system.


    At present, more than 50% of international employees in the company are R & D personnel and have a complete independent R & D system. They have a considerable number of patents in the areas of distributed network, blockchain technology, storage technology and data mining. In the global blockchain, the total number of patent applications and authorizations in the enterprise ranks the top. The technical team of the project is led by a former QvodPlyer P2P core algorithm engineer and has an experienced R & D management team for blockchain projects as well as first-rate blockchain background investment experts and consultant teams with extensive hands-on experience in traffic distribution industry . Investment and consulting teams have attended or worked at Stanford University in the United States, the United States Silicon Valley Mei Ah Innovation Incubator, Taipei University of political science and artificial intelligence research center.


    互动网络国际有限公司成立于2016年，是目前领先的区块链应用服务提供商之一。互动网络国际一直致力于通过去中心化的设计以产生一个公开公平民主的自治生态系统，为亿级海量用户提供优质的服务，始终处于稳健发展的状态。


    基于区块链改善用户的互动体验是互动网络国际的使命。目前，互动网络国际把“给用户和开发者提供客观公正的创新型应用评价体系”作为战略目标。通过去中心化的设计，让生态内的用户持有基于区块链和智能合约的代币方式，利用其自身的互动行为为应用进行“投票”和消费，以产生一个公开公平民主的自治生态系统。


    目前，互动网络国际50%以上员工为研发人员，拥有完善的自主研发体系，在分布式网络、区块链技术、存储技术、数据挖掘等方向都有了相当数量的专利，在全球区块链企业中专利申请和授权总量位居前列。项目的技术团队由前快播P2P核心算法工程师主导，并拥有一支经验丰富的区块链项目研发管理团队，以及一流区块链背景投资专家与顾问团队，具有丰富的流量分发行业实操经验。投资及顾问团队都曾就读或供职于美国斯坦福大学、美国硅谷美亚创新孵化器、台北政治大学人工智慧经济学研究中心等。
